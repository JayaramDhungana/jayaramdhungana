Nestled within the picturesque embrace of Nepal's Kathmandu Valley lies the tranquil charm of Shakharapur Municipality-1. Here, amidst the breathtaking Himalayan backdrop, the municipality exudes an enchanting allure that captivates the soul. Surrounded by the serene beauty of nature, Shakharapur Municipality-1 offers a sanctuary from the hustle and bustle of city life. With its ancient temples, monasteries, and bustling markets, it is a vibrant tapestry of culture and tradition. Every sunrise brings with it the promise of new beginnings, while every sunset paints the sky in hues of gold and crimson, casting a spell of enchantment over the land. Shakharapur is a place where the past and present harmoniously coexist, creating a timeless atmosphere that invites exploration and discovery. In Shakharapur Municipality-1, the rhythm of life unfolds at its own pace, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of Nepalese heritage and natural beauty. It is a place where every corner tells a story and every moment is a celebration of life's infinite possibilities.

About My Life

My name is Jayaram Dhungana, and I'm a dedicated educator and aspiring computer scientist based in Nepal. Currently enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (B.Sc. CSIT) program at Tribhuvan University (TU), I am deeply passionate about the intersection of technology and education. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am actively engaged as a teacher at Arunodaya Secondary School, where I play a pivotal role in shaping the minds of young learners. Specializing in teaching class 9 OPT mathematics and science, I strive to create dynamic and interactive learning experiences that inspire curiosity and critical thinking among my students. My journey as an educator is driven by a profound belief in the transformative power of education. I see teaching not only as a profession but as a calling—a platform through which I can empower the next generation with the knowledge and skills they need to thrive in an ever-evolving world. Outside of the classroom, I am deeply involved in ongoing professional development, continuously seeking opportunities to enhance my pedagogical skills and stay abreast of the latest advancements in the field of computer science. Whether through attending workshops, participating in conferences, or engaging in online courses, I am committed to lifelong learning and growth. In my spare time, I enjoy exploring the outdoors, reading literature, and engaging in creative pursuits. I believe in leading a balanced life that nourishes both the mind and the spirit, allowing me to bring renewed energy and enthusiasm to my work as an educator and student. As I continue on my journey, I am excited about the endless possibilities that lie ahead. With a steadfast commitment to excellence and a passion for making a positive impact, I look forward to contributing meaningfully to the fields of education and technology, both locally and globally.
